About me


I'm Dean Breed, I am a data specialist with particular focus on data science, analytics and visualisation for social good.

For nearly four years, I worked at Development Initiatives, specialising in nutrition and development finance. I also work across a range of other areas including humanitarian assistance, disability, and poverty.
